About us

The "pet protected" project aims to create a situation in which the residents of Israel distribute among themselves all the objects, related to animals, which they do not need, but are still valuable and it is a pity to throw them away. But to this day we have not had access to it. The project is a social business that sustains itself financially, and is also assisted by financial support of the good will of its users. As part of the course, we were given the tools to set up such a venture, as well as make a website accessible to all.

Environmental aspect

An object recycling situation will be created, which will slightly reduce the use of the earth's resources, and reduce the amount of garbage we throw away.

Economic aspect

There will be a situation of transferring unused resources from those who have them, to those who want them. It will also be possible for a person with financial difficulties to own an animal and have the ability to finance them.

Personal aspect

Lots of people come across situations where the products of their animals are already useless and sometimes they are even thrown away. Those people will have a simple and easy option to help others by handing over the items and feeling the sense of satisfaction.
